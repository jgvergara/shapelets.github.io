Our Team


We are a research and software development company with HQ in Malaga, Spain that operates across sectors offering data analytic solutions.

Shapelets was founded in 2018 by a small team of PhDs and software engineers who were previously working in a very ambitious project called Valo.

Our mission is to create the best in class machine learning open-source platform for time-series analytics. Our first release is Khiva, a library of efficient algorithms to analyse time series in GPU and CPU coming up from the latest research in academia.
